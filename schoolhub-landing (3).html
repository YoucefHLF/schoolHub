FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with SchoolHub.

How long does it take to set up SchoolHub?
+
Most schools are fully set up and running within 30 to 60 minutes. Our guided wizard walks you through every step, and our onboarding team is available at no extra cost to assist you personally.
Do I need technical knowledge to use SchoolHub?
+
No technical knowledge required. SchoolHub is designed for everyone — principals, teachers, and admin staff alike. If you can use a smartphone, you can use SchoolHub with total confidence.
Can SchoolHub work without internet connectivity?
+
Yes! Key features including attendance marking and student record viewing work offline. Your data syncs automatically and securely to the cloud as soon as your connection is restored.
What happens to my data after the free trial ends?
+
All your data is fully preserved. After your free academic term, simply choose a plan and continue. We'll send you reminders before your trial ends so you're never caught off guard.
Can parents access SchoolHub to track their children?
+
Absolutely. Parents receive real-time notifications via SMS or email and can log into a dedicated parent portal to view attendance, results, fees, and school announcements.
Is my school's data private and completely secure?
+
Your data is AES-256 encrypted, backed up daily, and never sold or shared with any third party. You own your data completely and can export or delete it at any time you choose.
